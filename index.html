Lima - Perú

Lima is the capital and largest city of Peru, located along the Pacific coast. It is known for its rich history, colonial architecture, and vibrant culinary scene. Lima is home to some of the world's top-rated restaurants, offering iconic dishes like ceviche and lomo saltado. The city also boasts historical landmarks such as Plaza Mayor, Huaca Pucllana, and the Cathedral of Lima.
